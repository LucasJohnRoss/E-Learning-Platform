1.Open Google Chrome
2.Navigate to the three dots in the top right corner, find the Extension section and open the Manage Extension Page
3.In the top right corner of the Extensions page switch Developer Mode on
4.In the top left corner click the Load Unpacked button, find and select the Extension folder stored on your local device.
5.Find the "Web-Page Blocker" extension in the centre of the screen and turn on the switch to allow the extension to run in the background.
6.Refresh the Extension
7.Open the extension in the extension menu in the top right of the toolbar
8.Optional-In the toolbar menu pin the extension for quick access
