Phishing

Phishing is the use of fraudulent messages to gain access to information you shouldn't be able to. Understanding how to recognise these attacks is vital for protecting yourself and your organisation.

Email Phishing

The use of emails to trick an individual into sharing information.

Smishing

The use of text messages to trick an individual into sharing information.

Spear Phishing

A targeted attack using personalised messages based on social media or organisation information.

Vishing

A phishing attack involving a phone call or voice message pretending to be legitimate.
